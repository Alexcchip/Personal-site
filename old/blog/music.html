What do I mean

Music always seems to evoke some sort of feeling for me. Depending on the artist and song, I will feel some sort of warmth within me when I hear a certain chorus or part of a song. It's almost as if I took some drug, and I am thrown back in time to when I first started listening to this song. That is what I will be talking about here.


LA LA LA LAAAA, ipod touch, Party Rock

My first memory of music was when my great aunt would take me in a stroller in midtown east (pre 2010) and sing to me this tune: "LA LA LA LAAAA" [will find the piece].

But I would say that my first true interactive experience (besides the walking pianos at FAO Schwarz) with music was on my ipod touch.
I remember having the itunes/music app on it and only having a few songs since I didn't want to pay $2.99 for a song. One of the songs, which seems to have been predownloaded, was Party Rock Anthem by LMFAO, Lauren Bennett, and Goon Rock 2011. These days, when I hear this song I don't really get thrown back to the early 2010s, but I've always found it to be hilarious that this song was seemingly downloaded by default.

Relationship with music

It wasn't till about 2021-2022 when I started to listen to music consistantly and when I started to form my actual taste. I think in middle school I was just listening to J. Cole for some reason. During my sophomore year of high school (2020-2021, remote school), when I wasn't rotting on Valorant or CSGO, I think I was listening to lo-fi.

During my jr. year of high school I had a lot of free time, and when I wasn't in class or working I was likely taking a nap in the library. This is the first example of a period in time that I am often thrown back to when I listen to the music of that time. While napping I would always listen to either Mac Miller or Nujabes.

Mac Miller & Nujabes


These days when I listen to these artists I usually get that warm feeling and it almost feels like I am in high school napping or speed reading a text for seminar or some magical realism latin american pieces from my Hinrichs class (favorite class at Bard). Or occasinally I get thrown back to that one time when Jawad and I synced our airpods andwalked along the hudson river greenway, that was really a wild night.
