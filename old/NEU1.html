Spring

During the Spring 2024 semester, I had the privillage to study aborad in London. This was a transformative experience for me, to say the least. I was not only able to experience how higher level math taught is like across the pond (Calculus 3, Linear algebra & Diff Eq), I was able to explore the beautiful city of London and several countries in both Western and Eastern Europe. The biggest difference between UK and US math was that in the UK, every concept was built from the ground up (often spending entire 1.5 hour periods proving a fundamental identity that is often taken for granted/brushed past).
